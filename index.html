Got questions?

What is Carbon Zero?
Carbon Zero is a buy-now-pay-later product that lets you purchase gadgets and electronics and pay in small installments at a 0% interest rate. These include phones, TVs, refrigerators, air conditioners, gaming consoles, and a whole lot more.
Do I have to pay any interest?
Not at all! That’s what makes Carbon Zero different; you only pay the original price and you also get to spread the payment conveniently.
Do I have to pay an initial instalment?
Yes, there is a small part of the total cost to be paid at the point of purchase. The rest is spread over a period of time, usually three months.
Are there any hidden fees?
Are there any hidden fees?There are absolutely no hidden charges when you buy any item with Carbon Zero. However, to help smoothen the repayment process, there is a charge on late repayments.
Can I purchase items from any merchant?
For the best experience, we only partner with authentic merchants. This means you can buy from Carbon Zero-verified merchants. But we’re always adding new merchants to our partner list to give you more options.
What items are available?
There are various gadgets and electronic items available on Carbon Zero, from smartphones to TVs, home theatre sets, refrigerators, air conditioners, inverters, laptops, and so on.
How do I apply?
You can apply by filling the application form with all the required details. Also feel free to search items from any of our merchant’s websites before completing the form.
Is Carbon Zero for Carbon Customers only?
No. It is accessible to both new and existing Carbon customers, meaning you can get access to zero financing by simply opening a Carbon account today.
Do I need to be a salary earner?
Carbon Zero is open to salary earners who have a verifiable source of income.
What is the minimum salary to be eligible for Carbon Zero?
At the moment, Carbon Zero is only available to customers who earn at least ₦120,000 monthly.
Can I apply for Carbon Zero if I have an existing loan with Carbon?
Customers with an active Carbon loan are not eligible to apply for Carbon Zero. But can go ahead as soon as they complete the repayment
How can I split my payments with Carbon Zero?
You can split payments for any product on Carbon Zero over a 3-month period but you can request to extend repayments to 6 months. However, this is subject to terms and conditions.
Is Carbon Zero available everywhere in Nigeria?
You can find our partners in Lagos at the moment. But be rest assured, we’re announcing more merchants in other locations across Nigeria soon.
Can I sign up as a merchant?
Yes, you can sign up as a merchant. You will go through our vetting process and, once verified, become an authorized Carbon Zero partner.
How do I become a merchant?
You can apply to become a Carbon Zero merchant by sending an email to carbonzero@getcarbon.co with the following information:


-Your business name
-The type of products you sell
-Your business location (we’re partnering with Lagos-based merchants for now)
-Proof that your business is registered in Nigeria
